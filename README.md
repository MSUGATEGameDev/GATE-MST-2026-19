# Shift
**By:** Max Mata

### Instructor Thoughts
The shifting wall transparencies is a really cool idea, understandably, having spent a decent amount of the time coding and coming up with new commands, there’s not a ton here, but we look forward to seeing what kind of cool layouts you can come up with now that you’ve ironed out the shifting mechanic.



### Tweaks we made post-class
One of the walls was missing some components and the coin didn’t have enough materials to make use of your 4-part material-changing script. So we turned those off because they were crashing parts of the game.

### Moving Forward
You will need to tweak the material changing script to deal with different models with different material counts. Other than that, build out the maze and make something cool. :)
